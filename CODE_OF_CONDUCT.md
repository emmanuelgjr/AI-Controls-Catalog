# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
as its Code of Conduct.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or
invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may
be reported to the maintainer at security@aicontrolscatalog.dev. All
complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
